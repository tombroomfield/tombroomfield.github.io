##Why Ruby?

I find it suprising how often I get this question. This may come as a surprise to many, given that a Rails Stack is the default for many startups around the world. Unfortunately however, but for a few notaable examples, Ruby generally sits on the fringe in Australia. I often find myself in discussions with .NET and Java developers about the benefits of Ruby - not to mention the pure joy it brings me as a software creator - and the overwhelming response is scepticism. Ruby (and Rails) continues to be thought of as hipster alternative ([See here](http://i.imgur.com/gyU0me4.png)). To be honest, this boggles me. [Rails recently turned 10](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=8085774).
